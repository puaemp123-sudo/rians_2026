Rethinking Intelligence:
A NeuroAI Symposium (RIANS)

Through the convergence of AI and neuroscience, new forms of intelligence are emerging. We invite you to join the “AI × Neuroscience” dialogue to examine how brain science inspires next-generation AI, and how AI serves as a powerful tool to advance neuroscience, fostering a cycle of mutual inspiration and discovery.
